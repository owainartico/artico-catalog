### Forecasting Logic Analysis (Spreadsheet Review)
- Installed Python 3.12 on Windows machine
- Analyzed 3 forecasting spreadsheets to understand current process
- **Key findings:**
  - Uses **full sales history** (not 90 days) to calculate velocity
  - Applies **monthly seasonality percentages** (Oct: 8.5%, Nov: 7.8%, ..., Jul: 14.5%)
  - Calculates stockout month = SOH ÷ monthly forecast
  - Lead time: 30 days mfg + 30 days ship + 30 days contingency = 90 days total
  - Buffer: +30-60 days on top (120-150 day total threshold)
- **Planning Groups discovered:**
  - Field name: `cf_discontinued` (API name) / "Planning Group" (label)

  - **FYTM:** 120 days (UK sea freight), default assumption
  - **China Standard:** 90 days, USD
  - **Main Runners:** High priority, never stockout
  - **New Ranges:** Low confidence, manual review needed
